# 📘 Bharat Enterprise - User Guide

> 💡 **Tip:** This system is designed to work smoothly even on mobile devices. You can run your billing directly from your phone.

## 1. Introduction

This billing system is built for small businesses to create invoices quickly, manage products, and track payments — even from a mobile device.

Designed to prevent common billing mistakes like stock mismatches and manual calculation errors, so you can focus on your business instead of worrying about software.

**Live Application:** [https://billing-software-sigma.vercel.app](https://billing-software-sigma.vercel.app)

---

## 2. Getting Started

1. Login using admin credentials
2. Add your products
3. Add customers
4. Start creating invoices

You can begin billing within minutes.

---

## Quick Workflow

**Add Products → Add Customers → Create Invoice → Done**

The system handles stock, totals, and records automatically.

---

## 3. Creating an Invoice

Creating a bill is as simple as talking to a shopkeeper:

1. Go to **"New Invoice"**
2. Select your **Customer**
3. Search and add **Products**
4. Enter the **Quantity**
5. Click **Save**

✅ Stock is automatically adjusted — no manual tracking required.

---

## 4. Editing an Invoice (Smart & Safe)

You can safely edit any invoice at any time without worrying about stock mistakes.

The system automatically adjusts your inventory based on your changes:

* **Increase quantity** → stock decreases
* **Decrease quantity** → stock increases
* **Remove a product** → stock is restored

💡 This prevents common billing errors where stock becomes incorrect after editing invoices.

👉 You don’t need to manually fix stock — the system handles everything accurately.

---

## 5. Returns (Credit Notes)

To process returned items:

1. Open the original invoice
2. Click **"Create Return"**
3. Select items and quantity

✅ Stock is automatically restored
✅ Customer balance is adjusted correctly

---

## 6. Customer Ledger & Daily Collections

**Tracking Customer Balances (Ledger)**
Go to any Customer's details page and click the **Ledger** tab to see their complete financial history, including invoices, payments, credit notes, and manual adjustments, complete with a running closing balance. You can also print a professional ledger statement to share with the customer.

**Tracking Daily Cash Flow (Collections)**
Click on **Collections** from the navigation menu to see a dashboard of your daily revenue. It breaks down total collections by payment method (Cash, UPI, Bank Transfer) and lists all incoming payments for any selected date.

## 8. Common Questions

**Q: What happens when I edit an invoice?**
→ The system automatically adjusts stock based on the difference. No manual work needed.

**Q: Will my old invoices break if I change products later?**
→ No. All invoices are saved as snapshots and remain unchanged.

**Q: Do I need to manage batches?**
→ No. Batches are optional and only for display. You only need to manage total quantity.
